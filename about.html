Our Story

Grown with Care,

Freshly started as a small Saturday farmers' market stall in 2018. Today, we partner with over 60 local farms to bring premium, organic groceries directly to thousands of families across the country.

How It Started

A Weekend Stall Became a Movement

Our founder, Ama Asante, grew up watching her grandmother negotiate directly with farmers at dawn markets in Kumasi. Frustrated with the poor quality of supermarket produce, she set up a small stand selling handpicked organic vegetables in 2018.

Word spread quickly. Within a year, she had a delivery route. Within three, a team of 40, a network of 60+ farms, and a mission: make premium, honest food accessible to everyone.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Two principles that guide every decision we make — from sourcing to delivery.

Our Mission

To make clean, organic, and locally sourced food accessible to every household — without the premium hassle. We believe what you put on your table should be honest, transparent, and genuinely good for you and the planet.

✅ Farm-direct sourcing
✅ Zero hidden additives
✅ Fair farmer pay

Our Vision

A world where every family, regardless of income or location, has access to nutritious, farm-fresh food. We envision a food system where farmers are celebrated, consumers are educated, and packaging leaves no footprint.

🌍 National coverage by 2027
♻️ Carbon-neutral by 2026
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Radical Transparency

Every product shows its farm of origin, harvest date, and full ingredient list — always.

02

Community First

We prioritize local farms and reinvest 5% of all profits into small-holder agricultural support programs.

03

Planet Positive

All packaging is compostable or recyclable. Our delivery fleet runs on electric and clean-fuel vehicles.

04

Zero Compromise

We turn down products that don't meet our quality bar — even when it costs us. Your trust is non-negotiable.

05

Delicious Matters

Healthy food should taste extraordinary. Our culinary team taste-tests every product before it lands on our shelves.

06

Customer Delight

We exist to make your grocery experience feel like a gift. From packaging to delivery — every detail is intentional.
